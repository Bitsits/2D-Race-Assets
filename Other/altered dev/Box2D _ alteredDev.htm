AS3 Physics Driving Simulation – phase 1

by Martz on Nov.11, 2008, under AS3

Car Physics

ok to kick things off I thought i’d show you a little demo of what i’m playing with at the moment. This is a car simulation based on the Box2D Physics engine for AS3 and im hoping it will blossom into a funky naviagtion system for alteredDev.com.

You can see it in action here!

Firstly a big thumbs up to all the guys at Box2D for their work on the AS3 conversion… so far it looks awesome! Also gotta put a mention in here to Android for the initial build of the car mechanics found here.
